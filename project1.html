ContainmentBreach

Starfield Custom Level Mod & Survival Mechanics

Project Introduction

ContainmentBreach is a highly intense, combat-focused custom level developed using the Starfield Creation Kit. Inspired by classic wave-based survival modes, the level traps the player in a heavily fortified facility where they must manage resources, optimize movement, and survive against escalating waves of enemies.

My Contributions

  • Level Layout & Greyboxing: Designed the spatial flow of the facility to encourage circular movement and prevent player camping.
  • Papyrus Scripting: Authored custom Papyrus scripts to handle iterative wave control logic and dynamic enemy spawning.
  • Custom Upgrade Stations: Implemented interactive weapon tier upgrade mechanics to reward player progression during the survival loop.
  • Combat Balancing: Adjusted enemy spawn rates, health scaling, and ammo distribution to ensure a challenging yet fair combat pacing.

Design Analysis

The core design philosophy of ContainmentBreach revolves around "Combat Loops and Spatial Tension." By strategically placing chokepoints and vertical transition areas, the player is constantly forced to evaluate their positioning.

I utilized the Creation Kit's NavMesh tools to ensure AI enemies flank the player effectively. The wave logic was iterated multiple times through Papyrus to create distinct "rest phases" versus "chaos phases," allowing for narrative pacing even within a pure survival setting.
