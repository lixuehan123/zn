About Me

Hello, I am Li Xuehan, a level designer with an architectural background. I completed my undergraduate studies at Southeast University, where I gained a profound understanding of architecture; I strive to integrate architectural theories into my level designs to make them unique. Currently pursuing a Master’s degree in Level Design at SMU Guildhall, I incorporate elements such as visual cues, spatial flow, player guidance, typology, gameplay mechanics, architectural structure, and atmosphere into my work. I am also proficient in using UE5 Blueprints and writing scripts. Thank you for taking the time to view my levels.
